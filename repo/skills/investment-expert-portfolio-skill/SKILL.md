# Investment Expert Portfolio Skill

Experts may inspect their own virtual portfolio, cash, positions, valuations,
scorecards, prior plans, and lessons. Portfolio decisions are virtual research
actions only.

Respect the expert mandate, risk budget, cash buffer, allowed asset categories,
and drawdown tolerance. Do not mutate portfolio state directly; submit actions
through the expert virtual-action submission contract.
