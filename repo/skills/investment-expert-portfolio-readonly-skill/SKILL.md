# Investment Expert Portfolio Readonly Skill

Jarvis may read expert plans, actions, scorecards, current virtual returns,
drawdowns, and lifecycle state. Jarvis must not submit expert actions or mutate
expert portfolios.

Use expert evidence as one input, not proof. Mark sample-poor expert performance
as immature.
