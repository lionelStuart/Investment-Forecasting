# Investment News Evidence Skill

Retrieve news through bounded search filters such as date range, asset, theme,
event type, sentiment, source, and keyword. Do not request or paste unbounded
news dumps into a prompt.

News is supporting evidence only. Cite news evidence IDs when news changes an
action, confidence, risk warning, or watch trigger.
